About DetectAI

Understanding the core technology, workflows, and permissions driving the platform.

System Overview

Defect DetectAI is an advanced computer vision system designed to automate quality control in manufacturing.

By leveraging state-of-the-art machine learning models, the system inspects parts in real-time, identifying defects such as scratches, dents, and irregularities with high precision. This ensures that only high-quality products proceed to the next stage of the production line.

Technical Highlights

Precision-First Approach:

We have calibrated the AI model with a strict CONFIDENCE_THRESHOLD of 0.85. This high threshold is designed to:

  • Minimize False Positives (incorrectly flagging good parts as defective).
  • Ensure that when a defect is flagged, it is with a high degree of certainty.
  • Reduce manual review workload by filtering out low-confidence "noise".

Verifying AI Judgments:

Even the best AI can miss subtle defects. Our workflow incorporates a crucial manual verification step:

  1. The AI inspects the part and assigns a preliminary status (Defective/Non-Defective).
  2. An Admin reviews the result in the standard Inspection Detail view.
  3. If a subtle scratch was missed (False Negative), the Admin can manually toggle the status to 'Defective'.
  4. This override is logged, ensuring 100% quality assurance before the part is shipped.

Smarter Over Time:

The system is built to improve with use through an AutoML feedback loop:

  • Manually verified images (especially corrections) are automatically tagged as "High-Value Training Data".
  • These images are collected to retrain the model, specifically targeting the types of defects the AI initially missed.
  • This creates a self-reinforcing cycle of improvement, increasing accuracy for future inspections.
